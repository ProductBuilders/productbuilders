## üéØ Marketing Strategy on a Startup Budget

<div class="content-box">

### 1Ô∏è‚É£ Pre-Launch Marketing

- **Create a Pre-Launch and Post-Launch Marketing Strategy**  
  Outline your objectives, target audience, key messaging, and channels for both phases.

- **Define Your Target Audience and Key Messaging**  
  Identify who your ideal users are and craft messages that resonate with their needs and pain points.

- **Plan Your Launch Timeline and Milestones**  
  Set clear dates for pre-launch activities, launch day, and post-launch follow-ups to maintain momentum.

- **Build Anticipation Through Waitlists and Early Access**  
  Use tools like [Product Hunt's Upcoming Pages](https://www.producthunt.com/upcoming) to gather early interest and feedback. Creating an upcoming page well before your launch can help collect emails of interested users, some of whom may become beta testers or early adopters.

These platform transforms text prompts into branded videos, making professional-quality video creation fast, easy, and budget-friendly.

## üìà Growth Metrics & Analytics

<div class="content-box">

### Key Metrics to Track

- **User Acquisition Cost (CAC)**  
  Measure the average expense of acquiring a new customer.

- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**  
  Estimate the total revenue expected from a customer over their engagement period.

- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**  
  Monitor the consistent monthly income generated by your product.

- **Churn Rate**  
  Track the percentage of users who stop using your product over a given time.

- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**  
  Measure customer loyalty and satisfaction by surveying how likely users are to recommend your product.

</div>
